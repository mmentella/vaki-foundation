# Known Public Manifestations

This document identifies known public Manifestations of the VAKI Foundation. The list is informational and non-exhaustive. Absence from it does not imply incompatibility or illegitimacy, and inclusion does not imply certification, proof of semantic conformance, or scientific, technical, commercial, or operational endorsement. Compatibility may evolve as the Foundation and Manifestations evolve.

The [Foundation Specification](FOUNDATION_SPEC.md) owns the semantic relationship: the Foundation defines invariant ideas, while Manifestations inherit, interpret, and implement those ideas within a domain. Manifestations remain independent repositories and choose their own tools, technologies, architectures, procedures, and domain-specific implementation details. They may extend the Foundation locally, but local extensions do not automatically become Foundation concepts.

The Foundation may identify known Manifestations without becoming their release manager or implementation or operational owner. This listing creates no submission, approval, compliance, certification, or promotion workflow.
